How To Order A Custom Cake

PLEASE REQUEST CAKES AT LEAST 2 WEEKS IN ADVANCE

  1. Fill out an inital contact us form with a brief description of the cake. We will contact you within 2 business days with a price estimate and to make an appointment.
  2. Come to an appointment to meet us in the store to talk to the baker more about what you would like!
  3. Pick up your cake before your even or have us deliver! Enjoy!
